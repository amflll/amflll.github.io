Working towards a zero-waste campus

 

In the Spring of 2015, the University was considering removing its compost bins from the Bear's Den cafeteria.  Contamination rates were above acceptable levels as most students did not properly separate their waste.

 

To address the long-term issue of waste literacy, our team developed an educational program to instill a culture of sustainable habits in all students.

 

The Green Module is a mandatory online education course for all incoming Freshmen. Based on the concept for Alcohol-wise, an online alcohol abuse prevention course, the Green Module is an interactive program that assists users in making sustainable decisions regarding waste management and energy consumption in college. In addition the module features simulations of sustainable campus operations to acclimatize students with the various facilities.
