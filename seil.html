Building a social entrepreneurship ecosystem.

 

In 2018 a trans-disciplinary seminar of graduate students from Washington University in St. Louis collaborated with FLOURISH STL, Generate Health, and FOCUS Health Impact fellows to develop disruptive transportation solutions to address racial inequities in infant mortality. To showcase their research and develop a strategy to implement their recommendations the "Convening for Action: Social Innovation Pitch Day" was organised.

 

A one pager was designed to explain the process of the Social Entrepreneurship and Innovation Lab as well as attract new community partners. In addition a new page was added to the website to share the data collected by students, to further the impact of the projects started in the class.
