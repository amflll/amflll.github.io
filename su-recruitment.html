"With 12 different branches, whatever you're interests are there's a place for you in Student Union."

 

Every Fall and Spring semesters, several Student Union branches conduct a recruitment exercise. To increase efficiency and achieve better results, a unified recruitment strategy was developed.

  • A single application was used to streamline the dissemination of information and emphasize a unified brand identity
  • Facebook  advertising was used to target WashU students with digital flyers that showcased officers from the different branches of Student Union. Featuring officers not only immersed potential applicants in the various aspects of being in Student Union, but also tapped into the officer's network of friends to increase post engagement.
  • Recruitment data collected from existing SU officers and previous recruitment cycles revealed that 45.6% of all applicants heard about the open positions through a friend that was an existing SU officer, compared to 26.3% from Facebook and 15.8% from the all-school emails. Thence recruitment cards were created, to encourage and facilitate conversations about joining Student Union.

 

Accomplishments:

  • Most contested First Year Class Council elections
  • 132 applications for 34 positions, beating the previous record of 2.7 applicants for every position available for Fall 2016 recruitment cycle
