AMELIA FONG

Amelia is a creative problem-solver, human-centered designer, and a collaborative leader.

 

Amelia is passionate about using design for social impact. She was the Vice President of Public Relations for Student Union from March 2016-2017, where she  implemented the organization's first digital recruitment strategy and balanced day-to-day communication needs with large-scale advocacy campaigns. She was also previously the Director of Community Outreach for the WashU chapter of Design for America where she helped develop user research strategies for studio members, as well as the Community Outreach Coordinator for Maturity and its Muse where she managed partner relations. She currently does freelance consulting work for non-profits in St. Louis.

 

Amelia is an alum of Washington University in St. Louis.
