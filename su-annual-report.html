The Student Union Annual Report highlights the work done by all SU entities in the academic year. Traditionally a document that has high readership from University administrators and low readership from students, I revamped the organization and formatting of the report to communicate information more effectively.

 

  • To commemorate the 50th anniversary of Student Union, 50 initiatives from all branches were selected
  • To increase readership from students, notable initiatives were selected and converted into shareable posts on social media.
